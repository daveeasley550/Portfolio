About Me

I am a newly minted, goal oriented, Full Stack Engineer with a passion for continuing to learn. I love working on a team and contributing to achieve a goal. My communication and interpersonal skills are excellent, coupled with my adaptability and leadership skills, allow for seamless transitions within developer teams and environments.

I recently retired from the Army after 20 years of service. While I was a Soldier, my main duties were as a Flight Instructor and mechanic on the UH-60 helicopter. I was a project lead for numerous major maintenance projects as well as creating doctrine and training methods for the units in which I was currently assigned. Problem solving and conflict resolution are some of my strong points. I think that is why I enjoy coding, attention to detail while managing time, coupled with different approaches to attain a common goal. I like to get things done.

During my time in the Military, I was afforded several training opportunities as well as difficult assignments. I thrive in high stress environments and really enjoy accomplishing the goals set for myself and my team. Doing so while never losing sight of interests of the people on my team.

My family and I have traveled and lived in several countries throughout Europe and the Middle East. Traveling is something that we love to do. While on our adventures, we like to really immerse ourselves in the local cultures and experience life as close to being a local as possible while still seeing all the sights. We feel that it is important to do so for our children to experience life outside of America. Diversity, charity, respect, and empathy are learned through experience and immersing my children in other cultures, has thus far, helped in molding them into outstanding future members of society.
